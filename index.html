Bio

I am a second year Ph.D. student at Gatsby under the supervision of Arthur Gretton. Prior to joining Gatsby, I graduated from Ecole polytechnique with a focus in Applied Mathematics and got a Masters Degree in Mathematics, Machine Learning and Computer Vision (MVA). I also worked as a Computer Vision Engineer at Chronocam. My work currently focuses on learning distributions. More details can be found in my resume.
